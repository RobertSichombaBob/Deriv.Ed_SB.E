About the Program

The CBU 6-Week Intensive Program in Mathematical Methods 1 and Economics 110 is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of fundamental mathematical techniques and economic principles through an accelerated, immersive learning experience.

Accelerated Learning

Complete two university-level courses in just 6 weeks through intensive daily sessions.

Expert Guidance

Learn from experienced faculty and teaching assistants with personalized support.

Practical Application

Apply mathematical concepts to real-world economic problems and scenarios.

Certification

Earn a certificate of completion from The Copperbelt University.

Learning Path Structure

Progressive journey through 6 weeks of intensive learning

Week 1-2: Foundation Building

Establish core concepts in algebra, set theory, and basic economic principles.

Algebraic Equations
Set Theory
Economic Basics

Week 3-4: Core Techniques

Master calculus fundamentals and their application to economic analysis.

Differential Calculus
Integral Calculus
Market Analysis

Week 5-6: Application & Review

Apply learned concepts to complex problems and prepare for final assessment.

Problem Solving
